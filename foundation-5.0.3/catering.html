We've got you covered

Love Jack Allen's Kitchen's food, drinks and easy going vibe in the dining room? If so, you are in luck because now you can enlist his expert culinary team to create a custom tailored, seasonally inspired menu for your next event. You can expect the same friendly, professional service, heartwarming food and Texas hospitality found in the dining room with JAK's catering team.

When the JAK's team steps out of the kitchen, the goal is to make each affair a memorable, carefree occasion with fresh, local and craveable cuisine. Bring us any and all of your catering needs—whether its a simple spread of your JAK's favorites for a holiday meal, or a multi course, seated dinner with a big group of family and friends—the Jack Allen's Kitchen catering team have you covered for all occasions.
